ğŸ“Š What Are Environmental Factors?

Environmental factors are external events and conditions that impact stock prices but are outside a company's direct control. They include:

1. Economic Factors: Interest rates, inflation, GDP growth

2. Commodity Prices: Oil, gold, steel, natural gas

3. Geopolitical Events: Wars, conflicts, tensions

4. Policy Changes: Tax laws, regulations, trade agreements

5. Currency Movements: Exchange rates (USD/VND)

6. Global Events: Pandemics, natural disasters, tech wars
